Letter Keys

 
NOTE: The alt key is used with the Alphabet modes to implement the corresponding Keymaps. See the Alphabet Menu for the available alphabets. Alt combines with Shift and Ctrl for various related symbols as well depending on the Keymap. Here, a Keymap is any mapping of keys to Unicode characters associated to a named Alphabet mode, registered in the Alphabets.data file.

SPACEBAR: Alt-SPACEBAR is like a Shift-right_mouse click on a word or highlighted text, bringing up the Dictionary/Thesaurus menu for that word or phrase. Alt-Shift-SPACEBAR is like Alt-Shift-right_mouse on a word or highlighted text, bringing up a Dictionary dialog box for entering dictionary entries for that word or phrase.

 
BACKSPACE: Alt-BACKSPACE deletes previous word.
